Pitt Hacks 2020

Pitt Hacks is a two-day hackathon for students in grades 8-12 in the Pitt-Greenville area interested in learning about coding, cybersecurity, and a varity of other computer science related fields. We welcome all skill levels from students who don't know the next thing about a for loop to those building their own machine learning algorithms and everyone in between. And the best part, it's totally free!
